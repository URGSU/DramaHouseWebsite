  Martin Cozens is pursuing a double major in Political Science and Brain / Cognitive Sciences as well as a minor in English with a concentration in theatre. Some of his passions include listening to countless hours of music (especially classic rock, blues, jazz, and classic crooners & singers), indulging in fine art and literature, remaining vocal and supportive of political activism, singing, creative writing, and enjoying an evening with a good old movie. Furthermore, Martin enjoys living with and sharing a fair dose of reality, remaining honest and trustworthy, and opening up new doors of possibility and perception. Despite delusions of grandeur, Martin is aspiring towards law school where he hopes to join the elite ranks of social crusaders whose wisdom of the law restores justice, honesty, and peace to a world gone out of control.
