Nick Potter

Logistics Officer

The Miraculous Life of Nicholas Potter~ Once upon a time, in a small town in France, a boy named Nick Jonas was born. He was the perfect child, until one day, his sister’s child was close to death and Nick stole a loaf of bread for his starving family. Of course, he was arrested and sent to jail. At the beginning of his tenth year, the jail became overcrowded and it was announced that some of the convicts would have to travel across the sea to America. Nick stood up and said “I volunteer!” Nick was loaded onto a large ship along with many civilians and zoo animals, and they set off for America. On the fourth day of the journey, while the captain wasn’t looking, the ship hit an iceberg! Somehow, Potter ended up in a lifeboat with a Bengal tiger. This is how he made the voyage to America, with only his philosophical thoughts and the tiger for company. Upon reaching America, Nick was put into an orphan boys’ home. The tiger was deported because there are no cats in America. It was a hard-knock life for little orphan Nick. At meal times, he always asked if he could have some more, but he was always laughed away. After a year, he was adopted by an old couple who asked him to call them Auntie Em and Count Olaf. This is how Nick Jonas became Nick Potter, later known simply as Potter. The couple already had a son named Taylor who attended Hogwarts at the time and would grow up to run his own human farm. They later gained a sister named Christina, who became a famous pop star. Nick’s brother Taylor had a best friend named Danica who became a close friend to Nick and the rest of the family as well. She was like the perfect child they never had. The Potters did have one more child: the aptly named Sir Not Appearing in This Biography. Nick and a friend were playing in the creek one day when his friend discovered a small golden ring. They fought over who should keep the ring, and Nick came out victorious. The whereabouts of his friend are still unknown. When Nick was about sixteen, he was on a field trip to a science lab and got separated from his class when using his new ring to disappear. He ended up being bitten by a radioactive spider that was on display. On his way home after the field trip, Nick witnessed his uncle being shot by a mugger. Nick approached the mugger and said “My name is Nick Potter. You killed my father. Prepare to die,” and then unleashed his new spidey powers on the man. The man did not actually die, but that was how Nick discovered he had the ability to fight crime Besides being a superhero, and of course, stage managing, Nick likes to spend his time playing barnyard pool, helping out on his brother’s farm, playing in Sgt. Pepper’s Lonely Hearts Club Band, drinking water, and being awesome. Around the holidays, Nick is very generous. He likes to leave many secret gifts, such as coins in a pair of shoes. On Wednesdays, he wears pink. Many people don’t realize that Nick is actually a well-published author; his works include The Bro Code and The Playbook (both available at your local Barnes and Noble). He is also known for his work on the St. Petersburg Pardox.
